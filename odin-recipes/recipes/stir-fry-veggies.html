Stir Fry Veggies

Description

This quick and healthy veggie stir-fry is full of color, crunch, and flavor. Perfect as a side or light main, and easy to customize.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Bell Peppers
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Stir-fry garlic in sesame oil.
  3. Add veggies and cook until tender-crisp.
  4. Add soy sauce and optional cornstarch slurry.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
