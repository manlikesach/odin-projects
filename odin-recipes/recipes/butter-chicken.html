Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy Indian dish with tender chicken cooked in a buttery tomato-based sauce. It's mildly spiced and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato puree
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Butter
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook chicken until browned.
  3. Prepare the sauce with butter, tomato, and cream.
  4. Combine chicken and sauce; simmer.
  5. Serve with rice or naan.
